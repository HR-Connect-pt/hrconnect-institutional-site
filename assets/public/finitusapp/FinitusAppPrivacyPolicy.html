Privacy Policy — Finitus

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Finitus ("the App", "we", "our") is a life-progress tracker built around the philosophy of Memento Mori — a personal tool to help you stay mindful of your time. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

By using Finitus, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Controller

The App is developed and maintained by:

H&R Connect Company

Email: support@hrconnect.pt

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact us at the email address above.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Data You Enter — Stored Locally on Your Device

To calculate your life-progress calendar, the App asks you to enter:

DataPurposeWhere it is stored
Date of birth Calculate weeks lived and remaining On your device only (local storage)
Life expectancy Calculate estimated remaining time On your device only (local storage)
This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third party. It is stored exclusively in the App's local storage on your device.

3.2 Anonymous Analytics — Optional, Requires Your Consent

On Android, the App uses Firebase Analytics (provided by Google LLC). This service collects anonymous, aggregated usage data such as:

  • Screens viewed and features used
  • App session duration
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
This data does not include your date of birth, life expectancy, or any personally identifying information.

Analytics collection is opt-in only. You will be asked for your consent in the Settings screen. You may withdraw your consent at any time from the same screen.

3.3 Crash Reports

To identify and fix technical issues, the App collects anonymous crash reports:

  • Android: Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)
  • iOS: CrashKios

Crash reports contain technical diagnostic information (device model, OS version, stack trace) but do not include your personal data such as your date of birth or life expectancy.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — Request a summary of data held about you.
  • Correction — Correct inaccurate data (your date of birth and life expectancy can be updated directly in the App at any time).
  • Deletion — To request deletion of any analytics or crash data, contact us at support@hrconnect.pt.
  • Withdraw consent — Withdraw your analytics consent at any time in the App settings. This will stop future analytics collection.
  • Object or restrict processing — You may object to analytics processing by declining or withdrawing consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@hrconnect.pt.

9. Children's Privacy

Finitus is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data through the App, please contact us at support@hrconnect.pt and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Data Security

Your date of birth and life expectancy are stored only on your device using the platform's native local storage mechanisms (Android SharedPreferences / iOS). We do not have access to this data. Anonymous analytics and crash data transmitted to Firebase are protected by Google's security infrastructure and transported over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
